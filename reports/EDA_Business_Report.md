## Key Findings

### Finding 1
Customers with month-to-month contracts show higher churn.

### Finding 2
Customers with shorter tenure churn more.

### Finding 3
Customers with higher monthly charges churn more.

### Finding 4
Electronic check users show higher churn.

## Business Recommendations

1. Encourage yearly contracts.
2. Target new customers with retention campaigns.
3. Offer discounts to high-bill customers.
4. Improve onboarding experience.
